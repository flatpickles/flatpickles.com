---
title: Whole
date: 11/3/2021
type: poetry
---

it's lonely out there  
in all that empty space  
twisting, turning, wishing  
something from nothing

alone, a blank slate  
then a razor's edge  
divine, resplendent  
transforming one to two

something, exclaiming  
being, escaping  
from confines of nothingness  
fresh to the world
